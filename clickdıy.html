    WHAT IS DIY?

    “DIY” literally means producing something at home, changing or recycling an existing product, without the need to be an expert in a subject or without the help of any expert. The reason why this trend has become wildly popular in recent years, in my opinion, is the pleasure of being able to say "I did it". The fact that we live in the age of consumption is undeniable, but people have begun to value handmade products more. When we make a product ourselves instead of buying it, its cost decreases while its spirituality increases. That's why people started to be more interested in DIY projects. If you can make new things out of your old socks and old clothes by changing them yourself, this is DIY. If an item at home breaks down and you can do it yourself without sending it for repair, it is a DIY. Instead of buying pots, shaping empty yogurt boxes and turning them into flower pots is the best DIY project.
