    DECORATION IDEAS

    Let Your House Sing

    Your home is also like your business card. It shows what kind of person you are, what you are interested in, and describes you. If you are a house owner who is passionate about music, you can create a fun and happy decoration with many objects.

    You can get inspiration from music for furniture and decorative objects. Old musical instruments are great objects to use in furniture decoration. You can use musical instruments as furniture or decorative objects.

    One of the most suitable instruments is the drums. You can use the drums as a shelf or a coffee table. You can make wall objects, stools, clocks or shelves from records, and integrate musical notes into your furniture. Old guitars are also suitable for creating extremely stylish objects.
