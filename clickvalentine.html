    Cities We Live in Map Surprise

    This handmade gift project is for couples who have to live their love in different cities. You can make an incredibly romantic surprise for your distant lover with this handmade gift project. Tear off the pages showing the city you live in from the map and the city where your lover lives, and place them between a white piece of paper with a heart cut in the middle. Then, write your words like "You are always in my heart, don't forget that! I miss you so much" or write your longing words under the white paper and give it to your lover as a gift! It's a great DIY project that shouldn't be missed.
